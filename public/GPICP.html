GreenPoints ICP is a next-generation Web3 application built on the Internet Computer Protocol (ICP). It allows users to earn digital tokens GreenPoints for completing real-world recycling actions, while ensuring that people with disabilities are included through accessible interface design. Whether you are scanning a QR code at a recycling station or uploading a photo of your action, you're not only helping the planet you're also part of a fairer, more inclusive digital ecosystem.

Why It Matters?

Traditional recycling systems often lack transparency and motivation. GreenPoints ICP closes that gap by introducing verifiable proof of action, smart contract validation, and token incentives all while prioritizing universal accessibility. It's not just an app. It's a movement toward environmental justice and digital inclusion.
